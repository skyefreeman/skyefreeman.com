Today I splurged and bought a license for a video recording/editing tool called [Screen Studio](https://www.screen.studio). It aims to make video creation from content recorded from the desktop, easier. So far, I haven't been disappointed!

The learning curve was minimal, and after a few attempts at recording a screengrab with properly timed cursor gestures I was able to make something that looked passable.

Why did I spend $89 bucks of my startup money on screen recording software? Because I've come to the conclusion that [Swift Starter Kits](https://swiftstarterkits.com) needs proper product usage videos to improve the sales funnel. 

Default screen recordings look boring, and I don't have much video editing experience to make it look tight on a time budget. I see an $89 spend on video recording tools being a good investment, both for time and for increasing the quality of my sales pitch.

After all, why would you part with your hard earned cash without seeing exactly the software you're buying in action?
